*Psychotherapist-in-training, PhD student in Computer Science*

I earned a Bachelor's Degree in Psychological Science and Techniques of Cognitive Processes from the University of Sassari in December 2019. In 2023, I completed my academic journey with a Master's Degree in Clinical and Rehabilitative Psychology, graduating with honors from the University of Catania. During my training, I completed internships that focused on Lacanian psychoanalytic, cognitive-behavioral, and systemic-relational approaches.

Currently, I am an **Integrated Approach Psychotherapist in training**. Additionally, as a **PhD student in Computer Science**, I am engaged in research and application within the project *SiciliAn MicronanOTecH Research And Innovation CEnter* (SAMOTHRACE). My work involves developing Artificial Intelligence algorithms for high-tech wearable devices and researching cognitive biases in forensics.
